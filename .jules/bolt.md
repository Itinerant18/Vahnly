## 2026-06-08 - Hoisting Allocations and Conditional Contexts in Kuhn-Munkres
**Learning:** During the critical assignment phase (`SolveKuhnMunkres`), allocating O(n) sized arrays inside an O(n) loop creates excessive garbage collection pressure, leading to significant CPU overhead and memory footprint in dense spatial grids. Additionally, eagerly instantiating thousands of `context.WithTimeout` for driver candidates—even when no external ETA corrector is active—compounds CPU time and degrades batch processing performance.
**Action:** Always inspect nested loops in graph/matching algorithms for arrays being allocated on each iteration. Hoist them outside the loop and reset their contents instead. Moreover, defer context/timer creations until right before the RPC or network boundary that strictly necessitates them.
